FOUR Frankfurt

Accessibility
that reaches
new heights

2015 – PRESENT

Opening the city for all,
FOUR buildings at a time

In the heart of Frankfurt, a formerly inaccessible site is being transformed into a vibrant urban quarter that will redefine the city's skyline and reinvigorate its core.

Designed by UNS and realised in collaboration with HPP, FOUR Frankfurt introduces four high-rise towers, reaching heights of up to 228 meters, that will forever alter the city. These towers, with their subtle shifts and kinks, create dynamic spatial and visual relationships while optimising daylight penetration.

The project's primary goal is to reconnect this previously inaccessible site with the surrounding city. By integrating the listed facades of Junghofstraße and introducing new streets, passages, and public squares, the development creates a seamless link between the financial district, Rossmarkt, and the pedestrian zones of the city centre.

FOUR Frankfurt is designed as a 'city for all', blending residential, commercial, and public functions — a bold step forward in urban development offering a new model for mixed-use, sustainable and inclusive city living.
